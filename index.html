Kingdoms

Plants: are multicellular and consist of complex cells. ex: flowers

Animals: consist of many complex cells. They are heterotrophs, they dont make their own food. They are the largest kingdom with over 1 million species known. ex: cats

Protists: include all microscopic organisms that do not fall in any other kingdoms. They are made of complex cells, separating them from Archaebacteria and Eubacteria. ex: green algae

Fungi: include: mushrooms, mold and mildew. Most fungi are multicellular and consist of many complex cells. They digest their food outside of their body. ex: mushrooms

Archaebacteria: are found in extreme enviroments such as boiling hot water and thermal vents under conditions with no oxygen or highly acid enviroments. ex: thermofiles

Eubacteria: are complex and single celled.They are the most common type of bacteria. ex: Anthrax
